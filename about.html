Goals

Software developers work in a field that's constantly developing new technological advances. Setting goals that help them expand their experience can also help them improve their skills. Gaining new skills can keep a software developer interested in their field long term and help them develop new interests that may affect their overall career trajectory. Staying updated on new technologies and techniques can also improve the career opportunities available to a software developer.

Goals

1 - Improve soft skills

A software developer should have strong programming and coding skills, but it's also important to have strong soft skills to advance in the workplace. Soft skills can be things like:

  • Communication
  • Problem-solving
  • Creativity
  • Adaptability
  • Ethic
  • Time management
    • Working on these skills will make me a better colleague and employee. If i have strong soft skills, it can be easier to work with or be more productive, both of which are skills that can help them earn a promotion or a new position. By developing these skills, I can strengthen my skill set, ease workload and improve opportunities.
